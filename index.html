What I Actually Do

  • Build internal platforms: tracking, automation, BI, cloud-native services
  • Fix unstable architectures and reduce high cloud bills
  • Integrate complex or unusual APIs, legacy systems, and external platforms
  • Build and scale high-load backend systems and real-time pipelines
  • Provide technical decision-making for founders and teams without a CTO

Problems I Solve 

I’m often brought in at the zero stage to give a product its first solid technical shape, or later, when a growing system needs senior engineering direction to move past its limits:

  • No clear technical plan for a new product or company
  • Early-stage founders unsure how to turn an idea into a real architecture
  • Cloud costs growing without explanation
  • Systems breaking under load or behaving unpredictably
  • Legacy platforms blocking product or business growth
  • Teams drowning in manual work that should be automated
  • Marketing operations unreliable due to tracking or integration issues
  • No senior engineer to own the decisions that matter
